How to make pasta very quickly

Ingredients

  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • Yellow Bell Pepper
  • Uncooked pasta
  • 1 cup Milk
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Butter
  • Oregano
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the uncooked pasta
    2. - Make sure to save the leftover water
  2. When pasta expands and feels fluffy, continue
  3. Cut the bell peppers into small pieces
  4. Put butter in a pan
  5. Heat it up
  6. Wait for the butter to completely melt
  7. Put in the cut up bell peppers
  8. Mix them for around 3 minutes
  9. Pour the milk little by little
    10. - Also pour the pasta water in at this step
  10. Add some oregano, chilli flakes, and black pepper
  11. Stir for a little bit until sauce starts to boil
  12. Add in the boiled pasta
  13. Mix it
  14. Once sufficiently mixed, pasta is ready to eat!
